The honest part

A good fit,
honestly framed.

Slotted gives you a good fit in the right window — not a tour-van fitting. We show ranges, not false precision, because that’s what phone video can honestly support. When you’re ready to dial it in, confirm with a real in-person fitting. Until then, you’ll be playing a club that actually fits — instead of one that never did.

  • Bands, not decimals. Every number comes as a range you can trust.
  • Used first. The right club at a used-club price beats the wrong club at any price.
  • No black box. Each match explains which parts of your swing drove it.
